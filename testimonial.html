Reduce overall battery consumption: One way to manage battery waste is by reducing the overall consumption of batteries. This can be done by using devices with longer battery lives or by adopting sustainable practices like turning off electronic devices when not in use.

Use rechargeable batteries: Instead of using disposable batteries, consider investing in rechargeable ones. Not only are they better for the environment, but they can also save you money in the long run.
